Why Dars-E-Quran Madrasa?

We aim to revolutionise Islamic learning for children and youth of all ages. Gone are the days of being forced to sit through hours of uninspiring and laborious sessions in an attempt to be able to read the Words of Allah ﷻ and learn the basics of His Religion. We believe that Islamic learning should be inspirational and fun for our children and youth.Developing a lasting love for Allah ﷻ, His Messenger ﷺ, His Book and His Beautiful Religion that will stand them in good stead to face the challenges of life in a difficult environment.
